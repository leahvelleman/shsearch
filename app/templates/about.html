What is this?

The Sacred Harp is a 19th-Century hymnal that thousands of people around the world still sing from.

When you're singing with friends, it's easy to have a song in mind but forget which page it's on. Sometimes you can look it up by title. Sometimes all you remember is "it's in a minor key on a right-hand page and it has the word 'eye' in it." This search engine is for situations like that.

About this app

This is a thing. I made it. It's an example of faceted search. When the documents you're searching are very similar, like songs in the same book, you can customize the facets very closely.

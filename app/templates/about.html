What is this?

A search engine for the 1991 edition of the Sacred Harp! Other sites let you look a song up by words or title. But when all you can remember is "it's on a bottom-right page and it's got a birdseye in it," this one can help.

Try it!

Try a search! Here are some of the things you can do. Click the question marks for more information.

corn in valleys birdseye ?
47b ?
"and breathe" ?
"so la la la so la la la la fa fa so so la" ?

Or explore songs by key, meter, age, length, etc.

About this app

This is a thing. I made it. It's an example of faceted search. When the documents you're searching are very similar, like songs in the same book, you can customize the facets very closely.

Thanks to Lael Birch and Savanni D'Gerinel for technical advice, to the Brainfriends for user testing, and to Margaret Youngberg for library science.
